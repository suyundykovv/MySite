Emirates Stadium

The Emirates Stadium is the current home ground of FC Arsenal, located in Islington, London. Opened in 2006, the stadium features a seating capacity of 60,704. It is renowned for its modern design, excellent facilities, and the iconic "Arsenal" name etched on its exterior.
